Flow

Y2 S2

Brief: Aquatecture

Description: Flow is an underground living museum that highlights the importance of land use through many eras related to the waterway. The museum is placed in the underground level as an extension of Phayathai palace. It’s different from the current museum in that it’s a living museum people can experience through activities. The key concept is using the ground level to separate the timeline. In this area, water is the main factor that evokes the Ratchathewi area, but these days the importance of the waterway is lost. Flow is also created to bring back attention to the waterway.

Program: Rhino, Blender, Ai, Ps
