Invisible Boundaries

Y3 S1

Brief: Extrastatecraft: Infrapolitics vs the Critical Project

Description: Invisible Boundaries act as an infrastructure that invites wildlife back to the city area. This project uses ruins as an asset to reintroduce nature into the urban environment, focusing on rewilding city spaces. Instead of treating nature as decoration, it becomes part of daily life. The design redefines human-nature coexistence through invisible boundaries and four key strategies.

Program: Unreal Engine, Rhino, Blender
