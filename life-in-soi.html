Life in Soi

Y1 S2

Brief: Spatial Relations ; Context ≈ Concept

Description: Life in Soi located in Talat noi or Talak Kie is a historic neighborhood in Bangkok, famous for its mechanics, street art, street food, and shrine. The project questioned whether the area is public or not, does it serve tourists or locals, and how can we share these spaces. The installation serves as a physical divider between the two agents. It consists of a display that uses fabric and wires to cast shadows and showcase local life. As many tourists visit the area to experience the local culture. Thus, this device serves as a bridge between these two groups.

Program: Rhino, Blender, Ai, Ps
